Mission Rewards

After the mission, mark as directed:

Primary Mission

If you were on the primary mission, mark xp as follows:

  • {{data.xp_trigger}}
  • You made effective use of your investigative skills.
  • You struggled with issues from your stress or conditions.
  • You provided emotional support for a crew member.

Secondary Mission

If you survived the secondary mission, you mark 2 xp.

You may also need to mark harm or stress.

Skipped Mission

Agents who skipped the mission get to take R & R (rest and relaxation).

This marks a healing tick and clears up to 2 stress.

Downtime Activities

If you went on a mission, you can pursue one personal activity before your next mission.

Assigned Downtime

If the Quartermaster assigns you to a project, you don't have time left for a personal activity.

Seek Stability

Visit your source of stability to relieve stress.

Train

Mark 1 XP in a selected track (Insight, Prowess, Resolve, or playbook advancement).

You can mark 2 XP for a track when your crew has unlocked the corresponding upgrade.

Recover

Seek treatment for your existing harm. Either roll using the quality level of the NPC treating you, or take one stress and roll 0d.

Poor - 0d, Standard - 1d, Fine - 2d, Exceptional - 3d.

Mark a number of segments on your healing clock. 1-3: one segment, 4/5: two segments, 6: three segments, critical: five segments.

When you fill your healing clock, reduce each instance of harm on your sheet by one level, then clear the clock. Extra segments "roll over".

Crafting

Work on a personal long-term project to invent, build or enhance gadgets, weapons, apps, or spells.
